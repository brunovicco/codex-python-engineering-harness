# AI impact assessment

## Purpose and intended use

Document the AI-assisted or agentic capabilities in scope and the users and processes affected.

## Data and trust boundaries

Document permitted data classifications, prohibited data, providers, tools, MCP servers, and
external content that must be treated as untrusted.

## Human oversight

Document review points, approval requirements, escalation, rollback, and suspension criteria.

## Measurement and residual risk

Link deterministic tests and evaluations, record limitations, and identify the owner who accepts
the residual risk.
